# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Primary users are fashion-focused customers browsing and buying products quickly on web and mobile. They need clear product information, trust signals, and a fast path from discovery to purchase.

## Product Purpose
Mendeez helps customers discover fashion items and complete purchases with confidence. Success means users can browse, compare, and act without friction while the brand remains recognizable and premium.

## Brand Personality
Modern, minimal, premium. The interface should feel clear and confident, with balanced motion that supports feedback and flow without visual noise.

## Anti-references
- Generic SaaS blue-gradient styling
- Over-decorated glassmorphism-heavy UI
- Loud neon gaming aesthetics
- Bland corporate grayscale without brand identity

## Design Principles
1. Prioritize clarity over decoration in every interaction.
2. Keep a premium identity through restrained, consistent visual language.
3. Make primary actions obvious and easy to complete.
4. Use motion to communicate state and improve comprehension.
5. Maintain familiar patterns so the product feels trustworthy and fast.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Target WCAG 2.1 AA across all screens. Maintain keyboard accessibility, visible focus states, semantic labeling, and sufficient contrast for text and controls.
